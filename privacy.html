Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2, 2026

Hunter Disk Cleaner ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our website and desktop application.

1. Data Transparency & Safety First

Our core utility operates with absolute transparency. Hunter Disk Cleaner is designed to analyze and purge junk zones locally on your machine. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal files, scanned data, or system contents to any external servers. Your files stay entirely on your local drive.

2. Information We Collect

  • Website Analytics: We may use basic, anonymized web analytics to measure traffic and improve our official website interface.
  • License & Payment Processing: Payment and billing processing for PRO licenses are securely managed by our authorized e-commerce distribution partners. Your financial details are handled with industry-standard encryption directly by the processor.
  • Update Engine Check: Our integrated auto-update mechanism sends a safe request to GitHub Releases to check if a newer version of the installer is available. No telemetry or personal data is attached to this request.

3. Compliance & Global Standards

We respect international privacy laws, including the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) and the LGPD. Since we do not harbor personal profiling data, your right to absolute local privacy is intrinsically hardcoded into our software design.

4. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us directly at contact@hunterdiskcleaner.com.
